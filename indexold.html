About Weddings by Palladium
A dedicated programme

A Dedicated South Asian
Wedding Programme

Palladium Hotel Group operates a dedicated South Asian weddings programme, the only major all-inclusive group with a team exclusively focused on Hindu, Sikh, and Muslim celebrations at resort scale.

From a Haldi garden in Riviera Maya to a grand Baraat procession in Jamaica, our specialists craft every detail, from authentic catering to Mandap design, so your family can celebrate without compromise.

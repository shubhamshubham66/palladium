How Your Wedding Days Take Shape

Everything That Matters, In The Most Beautiful Wedding Destination

Day One
Welcome, Haldi and Mehndi
The first day is really just about arriving. The Haldi ceremony happens the way it always does — messy, loud, and over too quickly. Then the Mehndi night, where the evening stretches out and nobody's in a rush yet.
Day Two
The High-Energy Sangeet
An unforgettable evening of choreographed Bollywood performances, custom lighting setups, live entertainment, and a proper spread — held across our beachfront venues.
Day Three
Baraat and Wedding Ceremony
A breathtaking beachfront Baraat procession with full dhol band leads directly into sacred vows at a beautiful floral mandap. The moment your family has been waiting for.
Day Three
Grand Reception
Then dinner, toasts, dancing, and the kind of ending that takes a while to come because no one quite wants to leave.
